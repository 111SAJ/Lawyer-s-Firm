CRIMINAL LAW

Nomaan & Associate have experienced team to deal with most complex Criminal matters. We have been successful in getting bails, have successfully got discharged our clients and have successfully defended difficult Criminal Trials by getting our clients acquitted. Even at the Appellate stage we have argued and contested Criminal Appeals and Special Leave Petitions with precision in major Appeals including Murder. Our hard work combined with precise management of minute details have led us to be leaders in the field. We have been dealing with innumerable Writ Petitions including Writ for personal liberty and other related issues.

MATRIMONIAL MATTERS

Nomaan & Associate have the distinction of dealing with matrimonial matters with their expertise. We have dealt in maintenance, cruelty, Domestic Violence, Divorce matters and also have expert mediators in cases referred for mediation. We have been able to get our clients a satisfactory settlement through mediation.

CONSTITUTIONAL LAW

Nomaan & Associate are the leaders in the field and have been successfully dealing with Policy matters before Honorable Supreme Court of India and different High Courts. We have dealt with Policy for Haj Private Tour Operators/ Haj Group Organisers very successfully before Honorable Supreme Court of India with number of reported Judgements to our credit. With expertise in dealing these matters involving arbitrary decisions of the Government in Policy issues, we are the favourites for clients/litigants to agitate their grievance before the Honorable Courts. Solomon & T has been dealing with Constitutional Law matters with precision and accuracy and are undisputed experts for advice, strategy and litigation in all Constitutional disputes.

ARBITRATION MATTERS

Nomaan & Associate endeavours for creative and proactive litigation management process with aim and objective of reaching a negotiated resolution of disputes. We have experienced team of both International and domestic Arbitration and other forms of alternative dispute resolutions. Our Attorneys have represented clients in high stake arbitration matters as well as acted as arbitrators in matters relating to arbitration. We expertise in commerce, contractual, construction and building, finance. ADR provide a smart solution for those who prefer to avoid the litigation process.

TAX MATTERS

Nomaan & Associate have worked with a variety of clients multinational companies, small and medium enterprise and startups. We specialize in Indirect tax, GST, Customs, Central Exercise,Service Tax, Foreign Trade Policy (FTP), Value Added Tax (VAT) Central sales Tax (CST). The firm provides customized legal services and the taxation team helps in compliance and advises the clients. The firm regularly represents clients before adjudicating and Appellate authorities, Tribunals, High Court and Supreme Court of India.

REAL ESTATES

The firm has expertise in handling disputes in the Indian Real Estate Sector. We represent our clients before different courts in complex real estate disputes. There has been a sea change in Real Estate transactions and regulations after the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act,2016 and the establishment of the Real Estate Regulatory Authorities in each state. Consequently, the ability of buyers to initiate proceedings have also grown many folds. We have dealt with various commercial dispute and disputes involving individuals before the NCLT and NCALT.

PERSONAL LAWS

Nomaan & Associate is handling all kind of civil cases and framing strategy for better assistance in the litigation qua the family disputes. We advise and have a team for litigation centered around property and family related disputes. We specialize in matters related to inheritance and succession battles and are committed to handle disputes efficiently and offer a prudent solution.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY DISPUTES

Nomaan & Associate have vast experience in representing clients in Intellectual Property Litigation across various High Courts and before Supreme Court in matters concerning trademark infringement, copy right violation, domain name and passing off disputes. As a team we provide advice and support to clients in relation to protection and enforcement of trademark and copyrights.

COMPANY LAWS

Nomaan & Associate has a team of specialized attorneys for Company Laws and Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code,2016 litigation. Our team of attorneys and consultants have been advising and appearing for our clients and getting satisfactory results.

DRT/DRAT MATTERS
